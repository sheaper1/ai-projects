What makes it different

Three ingredients. One island.

Most vodkas hide behind flavourings and excessive filtration. Palma Vodka does the opposite — three exceptional ingredients, handled with precision, to let purity speak for itself.

I
Organic wheat spirit
A clean, neutral base with natural grain sweetness — no additives.
II
Mallorcan water
Soft, mineral-balanced island water that shapes the texture.
III
Flor de Sal · Es Trenc
Hand-harvested Mallorcan sea salt — the defining signature.
Tasting notes

Clean. Precise. Unmistakably Mallorcan.

Nose
Fresh and soft — vanilla with a gentle lift of black peppercorn.
Palate
Pleasantly sweet, silken and round, with hints of lemon peel.
Finish
Mineral and slightly salty — turning warmly peppery with time.
Our story

Proudly made in Mallorca

In 2018, Byron and Amber Holland set out to capture the true essence of Mallorca. From their organic craft distillery in the heart of Palma, they make and bottle spirits inspired by the island's landscape, ingredients and way of life. Every bottle of Palma Vodka is crafted from organic ingredients, hand finished in Mallorca and individually inspected to ensure exceptional quality.
