Our story

Proudly made in Mallorca

In 2018, Byron and Amber Holland set out to capture the true essence of Mallorca. In the heart of Palma, their distillery became the island's first organic craft distillery — every bottle made in small batches on a bespoke 500-litre copper pot still.
