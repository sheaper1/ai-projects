4. How to enter — free, no purchase necessary

There are two free ways to enter. No purchase is necessary and a purchase will not improve your chance of winning.

  • Instagram: post a photo or video inspired by Mallorca to your public feed or story, tag @mallorcadistillery and include the hashtag #TakePalmaHome.

Both methods are accepted as valid entries and you may use either or both. There is no limit on the number of Instagram entries you may post, but each person can win at most one prize per monthly window. Only one email entry per person is counted.

5. Prizes

Monthly prize: in each monthly window, one winner receives the complete Palma Spirits Collection — the current organic range (Palma Gin, Citrus Gin, Spiced Gin, Rosé Gin, Monastrell Gin and Palma Vodka) — delivered to the winner’s address. Grand prize: the ultimate Mallorca escape, with a total value of €3,500+, awarded to up to three winners drawn at the close of the giveaway on 31 December 2026. The grand prize includes return economy flights for two from a major airport in the winner’s country of residence, three nights’ accommodation in Mallorca, a Palma Gin distillery experience, a guided local tour and the complete Palma Spirits Collection. Travel must be taken within 12 months of the draw, is subject to availability, excludes peak periods (July and August), and dates are arranged by mutual agreement. Flights, transfers, insurance, meals and any other costs not expressly stated are the winner’s responsibility.

Prizes are non-transferable and there is no cash alternative. If a stated prize element becomes unavailable, the promoter may substitute an element of equal or greater value.
